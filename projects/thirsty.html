Wipe out a town to quench your thirst? Challenge accepted.

A stealth arcade game - you're a vampire, and you'll do anything to satiate your thirst... even at the cost (or reward?) of Springfield's destruction.

Level Design & Storytelling

Designed 5 levels that emphasized increasing gameplay difficulty alongside meaningful storytelling.

Thirsty takes place over a period of about 5 weeks (each level being 1 night of the week). You begin in Springvale before tragedy has struck, and every night you hunt down your meal(s). I designed the levels in a way that make your actions as Seb the vampire feel impactful, as the town falls faster and faster into ruin as unexplained deaths and missing persons flood the town (and rumors of a vampire).

Every night you play, more and more areas of the town get shut down. From the schools closing to protect the children, to all public facilities shutting down as people flood to the church for safety - each night tells the story of the people of Springvale, as well as limiting the areas to hunt down lone walkers at night (making the game more and more difficult as people are on higher alert). Newspapers, missing person signage and graffiti flood the town as people panic and fall into chaos.

Lighting

Designed the lighting visuals for each of the levels to enhance immersiveness and storytelling.
