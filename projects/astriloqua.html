Rebuild the Stars

A small narrative adventure game about rebuilding the constellations.

DEVELOPMENT:

JULY 2025

ROLES:

Director, Technical Artist

LINK:

lectroblob.itch.io/astriloqua

Galaxy Shader

Created a subtly animated galaxy shader for the night sky inspired by abstract oil spill art with star lines.

Constellation Construction

Created system and VFX for building, recording, and deciphering player-built constellations.

Building: The player is able to pick up and place stars in any formation, and connect them together to form constellations.


Recording: Constellations once connected are grouped together and given a collision box, allowing the player to select and record them into the log at a telescope.


Deciphering: In the game, different constellations link to different fortunes (which link to different story endings). The fortunes of the constellations directly relate to how the player builds them - based on amount of connectivity, closeness between the linked stars, number of stars, and more. For example, a single chain of stars will result in The Caterpillar, while a constellation with the maximum number of connections will result in the Web of Dreams. There are also special cases (such as the pentagram) that result in a unique log recording.


VFX: Created a niagara system for the constellation links between the stars.

Miscellaneous Programming

Created NPC system, branching dialogue, map assembly, interaction system, and coded player animation.
